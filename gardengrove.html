Overview: Garden Grove offers a suburban feel with easy access to nearby attractions in Orange County. It is known for its diverse community and cultural festivals.

Top Attractions:

  • Christ Cathedral (formerly Crystal Cathedral)

Fun Fact: The Garden Grove Strawberry Festival, one of the largest community festivals in the western U.S., has been celebrated annually since 1958.
