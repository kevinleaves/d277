Sacramento: California’s Capital City

Overview: As the capital of California, Sacramento is known for its rich history, especially during the Gold Rush era. The city features historic landmarks, vibrant arts, and a growing culinary scene.

Top Attractions:

  • Old Sacramento Waterfront
  • California State Capitol Museum
  • Crocker Art Museum

Fun Fact: Sacramento is known as the “City of Trees” due to its dense urban forest, making it one of the most foliage-filled cities in the world.
